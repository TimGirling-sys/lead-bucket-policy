Privacy Policy — Leads Bucket (Sales Navigator)

Last updated: 2025-10-15

What this extension does
Leads Bucket lets you collect lead details from LinkedIn Sales Navigator pages you visit (e.g., contact name, job title, company, email, profile URL), keep them locally in a “bucket,” and export CSV or send them to a webhook that you configure.

Data we access

  • Page content on sales.linkedin.com only when you trigger capture (icon/popup/auto-capture in the popup).
  • Fields extracted by CSS selectors: name, job title, company, email, profile URL (and any fields you add later).

How data is used and stored

  • Data is stored locally in your browser via chrome.storage until you delete it, export CSV, or send it to your webhook.
  • No data is sent to us or any third party by default.
  • If you set a webhook, the extension will POST your stored leads to the URL you configured when you click “Pour.”

Data sharing

  • We do not sell or share data.
  • Data may be sent to your own systems only if you configure a webhook. You control that endpoint.

Permissions

  • activeTab, tabs, scripting — to message/inject the content script on the active Sales Navigator tab and read the page DOM on capture.
  • storage — to store your leads locally and your webhook settings.
  • Host permission https://sales.linkedin.com/* — to read page HTML on that domain only.

Remotely hosted code

None. All code ships with the extension. The only network call is the optional POST to your configured webhook.

Security & retention

Data remains in your chrome.storage until you Clear, Remove, export, or uninstall the extension.

Your choices

  • Don’t press Capture (or disable auto-capture) if you don’t want to store a page.
  • Use Remove/Clear to delete items at any time.
  • Don’t configure a webhook if you don’t want data sent off-device.
